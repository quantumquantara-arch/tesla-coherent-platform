## 3. Scope of Collaboration

The collaboration focuses on three pillars:

### **A. Materials**
Assessment of Q21 Evercycle Alloy behavior under Tesla-grade machining, forming, and stress testing.

### **B. Energy Systems**
Testing energy-coherence models to evaluate stability, resilience, and efficiency characteristics relevant to Tesla platforms.

### **C. Timing & Control**
Analyzing whether the temporal-layer logic can reduce drift, improve synchronization, and increase fault resistance in Tesla control systems.

No proprietary Tesla internals are required for initial evaluation.
All Quantara models included here are non-sensitive and test-ready.
