### Tesla × Quantara — Stability Platform Financial Overview

---

## 1. Document Purpose

This financial report outlines three levels of projected impact for the proposed Tesla × Quantara stability platform:

1. **Conservative Case** — Standard R&D investment, limited-scope evaluation  
2. **Transformational Case** — Cross-platform adoption inside Tesla  
3. **Coherence-Economics Case** — Quantara’s κ-τ-Σ stability multipliers applied at scale  

Each model is built to support Tesla’s internal decision-making, budget forecasting, and risk assessment.

---

# 2. Conservative Case  
### “Feasible Partnership Scenario”

This scenario assumes:
- One engineering evaluation cycle per subsystem  
- Modest material trials  
- Limited prototype fabrication  
- No full-platform adoption until proven  

### 2.1 Estimated R&D Costs  
| Category | Cost Range | Notes |
|---------|------------|-------|
| Materials Testing | $1.5M–$3.2M | Alloy tests, stress cycles, thermal cycling |
| Energy Stability Evaluation | $0.8M–$1.4M | Bench energy tests, thermal drift studies |
| Control Stability Testing | $0.6M–$1.1M | Drift mapping, timing variance, fault propagation |
| Robotics Integration | $0.7M–$1.2M | Actuator tests, repeated-cycle stability |
| System Integration Simulation | $0.9M–$1.5M | FEA, CFD, control-loop simulation |
| Prototype Fabrication | $2.5M–$5.0M | Small-scale demonstrator |

### Total Initial Investment (Conservative):  
**$7.0M – $13.4M**

---

### 2.2 Expected Outcomes
- Identification of 3–5 high-value applications  
- 3–8% efficiency improvement in targeted subsystems  
- 4–12% reduction in component drift/failure rates  
- Foundation for larger-scale evaluation  

### 2.3 ROI Window  
**12–24 months**, depending on subsystem mix.

# 3. Transformational Case  
### “Cross-Tesla Adoption Scenario”

This scenario assumes:
- Multiple validated subsystems  
- Adoption in high-volume production  
- Long-term strategic integration  

### 3.1 Core Drivers of Value  
1. **Thermal stability** → longer component life  
2. **Control stability** → fewer recalibrations  
3. **Material stability** → lower fatigue & replacements  
4. **Fault-resistant architectures** → reduced downtime

|--------|------------|

| Chassis fatigue reduction | +8–16% structural lifespan |

Value range: **$450M – $1.1B/yr**
